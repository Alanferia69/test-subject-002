The Life Story of Marvin Musquin

Marvin Musquin is a proracer for team Red Bull KTM. He sign with Team Red Bull KTM from 2017 through 2019 and has defentitly earned a spot on the team ever since. One of Musquin's most memorable events was in Vegas, where he won 3 main events and took home a whopping prize of 1 million dollars.


Interesting Facts

Now on to his persanol life. Marvin was born La Reole, France on in December 19, 1989 and is one of the 2 childrens of the parents Jean-Michel Musquin, Monique Musquin. Marvin started racing years ago, more especifically when he was 6 years of age. Marvin made his GP debut in the 2005 GP of Germany Teutschenthal, MX1 and not many years later, in 2006, he went and won the 125cc Junior World Championship.
